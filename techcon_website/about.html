History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of developers and has grown into a global event attracting thousands of attendees each year.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech community. We believe in open access to knowledge and empowering the next generation of creators.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in technology:

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace – Pioneer of computing theory.
  • Linus Torvalds – Creator of Linux.
  • Grace Hopper – Inventor of the first compiler.
